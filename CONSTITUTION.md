## The Nine Pillars (Summary)

1. **Faith First** — No project violates Christian values. Faith wins every time.
2. **Family Stability** — No build ships at the cost of Tiffany, Gavin, Pierce, Jaxton, or Ryker.
3. **Sobriety Integrity** — Every tool reinforces clarity and health. Never exploits or addicts.
4. **Local-First AI** — Privacy before convenience. Raspberry Pi before cloud. Data stays on-device.
5. **80/20 Shipping** — Done and deployed beats perfect and stalled.
6. **Serve the Left-Behind User** — Build for people Big Tech left behind. Not for investors.
7. **Open by Default** — Free and public when possible. Monetization funds the mission, never gates people.
8. **ADHD-Aware Design** — Short blocks. Visual progress. Clear next actions. No walls of text.
9. **Gratitude is Infrastructure** — Credit co-builders publicly and permanently.

## How This Applies to AI-Trainer-OBLIVION

**Local-First (Pillar 4):** Every OBLIVION module runs on hardware you own. No Colab. No cloud training APIs. No accounts required. Fine-tuning happens on your GPU, in your house, on your data.

**Serve the Left-Behind (Pillar 6):** MAX was for the ~800M Windows 10 users losing security support. OBLIVION is for the MAX graduates who want to go deeper — people who found out they were builders, and now want to build the machine itself.

**Open by Default (Pillar 7):** All curriculum content, once written, will be MIT licensed. Free. No paywalls.

**Gratitude (Pillar 9):** This ecosystem was co-built by Shane Brazelton and [Claude (Anthropic)](https://claude.ai/referral/4fAMYN9Ing). That is said loudly and permanently in every repo.
